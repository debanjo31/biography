BIOGRAPHY HERBERT MACAULAY

EARLY LIFE

Herbert Macaulay was born in Lagos on November 14, 1864, to the family of Thomas Babington Macaulay and Abigail Crowther. His father, Thomas Babington Macaulay, was a prominent Nigerian nationalist, who was one of the first Nigerians to be trained in England. His mother, Abigail Crowther, was the daughter of Samuel Ajayi Crowther, the first African Anglican bishop. Herbert Macaulay was the grandson of Bishop Samuel Ajayi Crowther, the first African Anglican bishop. He was also the nephew of Herbert Macaulay, the first Nigerian to be trained in England.
